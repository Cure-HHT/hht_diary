# REQ-o80010: Training and Personnel Qualification

**Level**: Ops | **Status**: Draft | **Refines**: REQ-p90001-A, REQ-p90001-C

## Rationale

Personnel who use electronic systems for regulated activities must be trained and qualified. This requirement establishes training and qualification obligations for system users.

## Assertions

A. The organization SHALL establish education, training, and experience requirements for personnel who develop, maintain, or use electronic record/electronic signature systems.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(i)*

B. The organization SHALL document training completion for each user before granting system access for regulated activities.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 18*

C. The organization SHALL provide training on data integrity principles, including ALCOA++ concepts, for all users who create or modify clinical data.
   *Source: GCP Data - Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Training requirements*

D. The organization SHALL ensure training is role-appropriate and covers the specific system functions each role may access.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(i)*

E. The organization SHALL ensure personnel are qualified by education, training, and experience for their delegated trial-related duties.
   *Source: GCP Data - Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, ICH E6(R3) 4.2*

F. The organization SHALL maintain training records for the duration of employment plus the required record retention period.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 18*

G. The organization SHALL require retraining when significant system changes are implemented.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 29*

H. The organization SHALL verify the identity of an individual before establishing, assigning, certifying, or otherwise sanctioning that individual's electronic signature or any element of such electronic signature.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.100(b)*

*End* *Training and Personnel Qualification* | **Hash**: a6b89081

# REQ-o80020: Record Retention and Archival

**Level**: Ops | **Status**: Draft | **Refines**: REQ-p90001-F

## Rationale

Electronic records and their associated metadata must be retained and accessible for the required retention periods. This requirement establishes retention and archival obligations.

## Assertions

A. The organization SHALL protect records to enable their accurate and ready retrieval throughout the records retention period.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(c)*

B. The organization SHALL retain electronic records in accordance with FDA guidance for clinical investigations.
   *Source: 2024 10 FDA Guidance on electronic storaage 0 58358119fnl.pdf, Section II*

C. The organization SHALL ensure records remain accessible and readable throughout the retention period, including through technology migrations.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(c)*

D. The organization SHALL ensure long-term accessibility and readability of electronic records.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 26*

E. The organization SHALL implement periodic verification of archived record integrity and accessibility.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 27*

F. The organization SHALL maintain the ability to produce records in formats suitable for regulatory inspection.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(b)*

G. The organization SHALL document and test recovery procedures for archived electronic records.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 27*

H. The organization SHALL ensure audit trail data is retained for at least as long as the associated electronic records.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(e)*

I. The organization SHALL maintain validation documentation and evidence packages that enable customers to perform fit-for-purpose assessments of the system before use in clinical trials.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11.10(a); GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 8*

*End* *Record Retention and Archival* | **Hash**: 8d3a4a76

# REQ-o80030: Standard Operating Procedures

**Level**: Ops | **Status**: Draft | **Refines**: REQ-p90001-B, REQ-p90001-D, REQ-p90004-B, REQ-p90004-C, REQ-p90004-D, REQ-p90004-E, REQ-p90004-F

## Rationale

Documented procedures are essential for consistent operation of electronic systems and compliance with regulatory requirements. This requirement establishes SOP obligations.

## Assertions

A. The organization SHALL maintain written policies that hold individuals accountable for actions initiated under their electronic signatures.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(j)*

B. The organization SHALL limit system access to authorized individuals through documented procedures.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(d)*

C. The organization SHALL document procedures for granting, modifying, and revoking system access.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 19*

D. The organization SHALL document procedures for responding to security incidents including unauthorized access attempts.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 25*

E. The organization SHALL document procedures for backup, recovery, and business continuity.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 27*

F. The organization SHALL document procedures for system change control and configuration management.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 29*

G. The organization SHALL maintain distribution, access, and use controls over systems documentation.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(k)*

H. The organization SHALL maintain procedures and documentation that enable customers to submit non-repudiation letters to FDA certifying that electronic signatures are intended to be the legally binding equivalent of traditional handwritten signatures.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.100(c)*

I. The organization SHALL document procedures for the data correction process, including training requirements for personnel who perform data corrections.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 19; ISO 14155 J.2*

J. The organization SHALL conduct audits that evaluate the effectiveness of the data correction process.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 20; ISO 14155 J.3*

K. The organization SHALL establish risk-based audit trail review procedures that determine which audit trails and metadata require review and retention.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Reqs 14-15; ICH E6(R3) 4.2.2(e), 4.2.3*

L. The organization SHALL foster a working environment that encourages reporting of data omissions and erroneous results through data governance systems.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 22; EMA/INS/GCP/112288/2023 4.1*

*End* *Standard Operating Procedures* | **Hash**: 17e9b594

When electronic systems are hosted or managed by third-party IT service providers or cloud platforms, additional controls are required to ensure regulatory compliance. This requirement addresses organizational controls for outsourced system components.

   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 30*

E. The organization SHALL ensure customer audit rights for IT service providers as required by GCP.

# REQ-o80050: System Validation Evidence

**Level**: Ops | **Status**: Draft | **Refines**: REQ-p90001-G, REQ-p90004-A

## Rationale

Customers using electronic systems for regulated activities must validate those systems for fitness for purpose. As a software vendor, the organization must provide the validation documentation and evidence packages that enable customers to perform their validation obligations.

## Assertions

A. The organization SHALL maintain validation documentation including test plans, test results, and validation conclusions that demonstrate accuracy, reliability, consistent intended performance, and the ability to discern invalid or altered records.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(a)*

B. The organization SHALL provide customers with validation evidence packages sufficient to support their fit-for-purpose assessments before use in clinical trials.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 28*

C. The organization SHALL maintain traceability between requirements, test cases, and test results within validation documentation.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(a)*

D. The organization SHALL implement change control procedures and provide customers with impact assessments documenting the effect of changes on the validated state.
   *Source: GCP Data - Consolidated Requirements for Audit Trails & Data Corrections - 02Sept2025.pdf, Req 29*

E. The organization SHALL provide updated validation evidence when changes are made that could affect system performance or data integrity.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(a)*

F. The organization SHALL provide validation documentation that demonstrates the system's fitness for intended use in the clinical investigation context.
   *Source: 2024 10 FDA Guidance on electronic storaage 0 58358119fnl.pdf, Section II*

G. The organization SHALL retain validation documentation for the lifetime of the system plus the required record retention period.
   *Source: 21 CFR Part 11 (up to date as of 1-22-2026).pdf, 11.10(k)*

*End* *System Validation Evidence* | **Hash**: 6a097678
