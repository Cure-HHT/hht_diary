## Executive Summary

Privacy regulations such as GDPR and HIPAA mandate transparent disclosure of data collection practices and user rights. Clinical trial regulations including FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ICH-GCP E6(R2), and EU Clinical Trial Regulation 536/2014 impose additional protections for research participant data. This requirement defines the multi-layered privacy documentation approach (general policies, diary-specific addenda, clinical trial templates, sponsor-specific supplements) that accommodates different user types and regulatory contexts while maintaining a consistent baseline of privacy protection.
