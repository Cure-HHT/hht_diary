B. In the event of a conflict between definitions or requirements among the Reference Documents, the following order of precedence SHALL apply:
- Project-Specific Glossary (this document)
- FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and associated FDA Guidance
- ICH E6(R2) (Good Clinical Practice)
- CDISC Glossary
- International Standards (e.g., ISO/IEC 24760-1)
- Technical Frameworks (e.g., NIST SP 800-63)

Where a conflict exists between a general requirement and a specific requirement, the specific requirement shall govern.
