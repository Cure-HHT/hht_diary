# Glossary Precedence

When a term is defined in more than one Reference Document, or when a
general requirement and a specific requirement disagree, the rules below
resolve the conflict.

## Reference-Document Precedence

In the event of a conflict between definitions or requirements among the
Reference Documents, the following order of precedence SHALL apply
(highest authority first):

1. Project-Specific Glossary (`glossary-core.md`)
2. FDA 21 CFR Part 11 and associated FDA Guidance
3. ICH E6(R2) (Good Clinical Practice)
4. CDISC Glossary
5. International Standards (e.g., ISO/IEC 24760-1)
6. Technical Frameworks (e.g., NIST SP 800-63)

## Specific Over General

Where a conflict exists between a general requirement and a specific
requirement, the specific requirement SHALL govern.
